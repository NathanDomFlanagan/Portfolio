Snake

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move the snake:

  • Up: W
  • Down: S
  • Left: A
  • Right: D

Press the 'E' key to pause/start the game.

Eat the red food to grow and increase your score.

Avoid running into the walls or yourself.
